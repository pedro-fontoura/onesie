I love hearing from you. Found a bug, have a feature idea, or just want to share a win? My inbox is open.

  • Suggestions: Ideas to keep the interface clean or add features.
  • Rule breaks: Spotted a logic error or an illegal move? Give me a heads-up.

  • The box list: Nominate a solo game for future implementation.

By sending, you're cool with me quoting your feedback (with credit) to help spread the word about Onesie.
